Biomechanics

  • Course Description
    This module includes the higher biomechanics with the material behavior of the soft tissue structures (passive structures (ligaments, tendons, ligaments, articular cartilages) and active structures (musculature)). Theoretical and scientific foundations on which digital human models are based are taught in practice-oriented ways.
  • Lecturer:  K. Rauner [L], V. Senner
  • Language:  German
